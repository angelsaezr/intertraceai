One sunny Saturday morning, Emma went to the park near her house. She wanted to relax and read her favorite book.
When she arrived, she saw a small puppy sitting alone under a tree. The puppy was shaking and looked very scared.

Emma slowly walked to the puppy and said, “Hello, little one. Are you lost?” The puppy wagged its tail but did not move.
Emma picked it up gently. It felt cold and hungry.

She searched around the park and asked people, “Is this your puppy?” But nobody knew anything about it.
Emma decided to take the puppy home. She gave it warm milk and a soft blanket. The puppy finally felt safe and fell asleep.

Emma made a sign that said, “Found Puppy” with her phone number. She put the sign at the park entrance
and also posted a picture online.

The next day, Emma received a call from a little boy named Tom. He said, “Thank you! That is my puppy, Max.
He ran away yesterday.” Tom and his mom came to Emma’s house. When Max saw Tom, he barked happily and jumped into his arms.

Tom smiled and said, “Thank you for taking care of him.” Emma felt happy that she helped. It was a
small adventure, but she would always remember the day she found Max in the park.
