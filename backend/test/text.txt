In a small village by the river, the mornings always smelled of freshly baked bread and damp wood.
Children ran through the cobbled streets, chasing kites made of colored paper, while the elders chatted
beneath the great oak that watched over the square. Sometimes, when the wind blew from the mountains,
it carried with it an ancient whisper, as if the forest itself wanted to share its secrets. No one knew
exactly what it said, but everyone listened in silence, with a mix of respect and curiosity.
